About us

Ain's Jewelry is a small business founded by a group of friends. What started out as a hobby soon turned into a profit making business. We promise You quality products and service and will do our best to satisfy Your needs.

General questions

Underneath this section we will answer the most asked questions about our online store. For answers not found in this page you can ask by going to the Contact us page, or directly by clicking here.

Where do you ship?

We are happy to inform you that we offer international shipping! The shipping fee varies depending on your location.

We ship to most countries, but unfortunately there are still a few countries that we do not ship to. To make sure of whether we ship to you, please ask us beforehand from the Contact us page.

How much does shipping cost?

For orders coming from Europe, the flat rate shipping fee is 7.50€. Orders from other continents have a shipping fee of 12.95€. Any order over 100€ is eligible for free shipping.

Happy shopping!

Return policy

We really hope you love your new product! But in cases where you want to return a product, here is what you need to know

We accept returns within 30 days of receipt. The products must be returned in their original packaging. We do not, however, accept returns for custom made jewelry. In those cases we can offer to modify the custom made product instead.

Lost or damaged goods

If your package is lost during transport or you have received damaged goods, we can either return your money or re-send you the same order.

The process of handling a lost package is significantly faster, since our packages come with a tracking number. The latter, however, has a longer handling time.
